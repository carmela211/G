story behind it's establishment

Last year, when vacation break finally started, I thought I could already have my rest break and have time to catch up with my friends. But, much to my surprise, certain unanticipated circumstances arose, shocking everyone. During the first week of August 2022, my family and I tested positive for covid 19 and we're quarantined and isolated for months. As a result, I was unable to enjoy our vacation and felt alone, as it was the first time I had been separated from my family. While I was recovering, I was able to go back to my first love which is writing calligraphy. By then, I was able to enhance and develop my calligraphy skills.

That event, I believe, was somehow a blessing in disguise for my family. After everyone had recovered, blessings from blessings arrived, and gawang gamat was founded in September 2022. At first, I wasn't about to sell my calligraphy works since it was just merely a habit and interest. However, some of my friends encouraged me to sell them, and unexpectedly, the items I sold were loved by many.

Art became my shoulder to lean on whenever I feel stressed and blue. It makes my life colorful, sparks my imagination, and gives me the happiness I've been longing for when I feel sad. I love how my business is about the thing I love doing. Whenever bulk orders are coming, I never felt tired since I enjoy doing it. It also helped me with my financial needs. I will always be dedicated to create products and crafts that will dazzle your eyes and will make you and your special someone blissful.
